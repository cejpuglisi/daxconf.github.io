About DAX

The DAX Conference will focus on data science, analytics, and general data exploration. Engineers, data scientists, analytic developers, system architects, and business leaders are encouraged to share their experiences and present a topic that would be of interest to the local data community. Expected attendees include engineers, thought leaders, business leaders, and professionals from local government, government defense and intelligence agencies, start-up companies, large data analytic and data science companies, and local universities.

Keynote
Kirk Borne - The Future of AI - Creating Value at the Convergence of Emerging Technologies

Data-driven applications are the norm in business, industry, and our personal lives. These applications increasingly engage various existing and emerging technologies, such as Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Autonomous Systems, Computer Vision, Digital Twins, Blockchain, Internet of Things, and more. The addition of AI to the data process flows enables the applications to be more than data-driven, but become insights-driven, intelligent, smart, and value-oriented. The future of AI will thus focus on value creation at the convergence of emerging technologies.

Data Science Track - Session 1
Roshan Punnpoose & Ope Arowojolu - Compliance Policy Document Scoring using Natural Language Processing

As cyber security attacks increase exponentially across businesses and national infrastructure, compliance is becoming more and more important to gauge and track. In the past, the compliance process has been mostly manual and time consuming. Utilizing the latest advancements in Deep Learning NLP, we have built an automated end to end solution to automatically process and score compliance policy documents. In this talk, we will outline our semi-supervised method to score various policy documents across different compliance standards.

Data Science Track - Session 2
John Muschelli - Streamlining Data: Tidy Data as a Service

Streamline Data Science is a company whose goal is to deliver tidy data as a service. Tidy data is not reliant on one person or spreadsheet - it's managed data that is cleaned and curated. It's the data that allows you to make data-driven decisions. We aim to provide an ELT service for most clients, with bespoke solutions for their "untidy" data. Our process is to take operations, especially manual manipulations in Excel spreadsheets, and turn them into an internal codebase that automatically returns your data in a tidy format in a data warehouse and/or pushed back to your internal data warehouse. We also deliver a high level dashboard and automated reports for clients.

Exploration Track - Session 2
Sean Mussenden - Investigative Reporting with Remote Sensing

An overview of how data journalists are capitalizing on the increasing availability of high-quality satellite imagery and remote-sensed satellite data to drive meaningful investigative reporting. Three examples: BuzzFeed News won a Pulitzer using a novel technique to identify Muslim prison camps in China. The Washington Post showed how neglected Russian natural gas fields were spewing climate-warming methane into the atmosphere. A project I worked on used urban tree cover data to show how poor Baltimore neighborhoods have less cooling tree cover than wealthier neighborhoods.

Kirk Borne - Keynote
Data Prime Inc. / Architect

Dr. Kirk Borne is Chief Science Officer at startup Data Prime Inc. since April 2021, and founder of his own freelance influencer marketing business Data Leadership Group LLC. He is a career data professional, data science leader, and research astrophysicist. From 2015 to 2021, he was Principal Data Scientist, Data Science Fellow, and Executive Advisor at Booz Allen Hamilton. Previously, Kirk was professor of Astrophysics and Computational Science at George Mason University for 12 years where he taught data science at the graduate and undergraduate levels. He has been an Adjunct Professor at the University of Maryland Global Campus and at the University of Texas in Arlington. Before that, he spent 20 years supporting data systems activities for NASA space science missions, including the Hubble Telescope and at NASA's Space Science Data Operations Office. He has a Ph.D. in astronomy from Caltech. Since 2013, he has been identified as a top worldwide influencer on social media, promoting data science, machine learning, AI, IoT strategy, and data literacy for all.

FAQ

General questions

What is the DAX Conference?
DAX is the annual premier event that serves as a focal meeting point for the data engineering community. The event is hosted and organized by the Data Works MD community. Attendees will include both newcomers and experts.
What is Data Works MD?
Data Works MD is a community of professionals, students, and enthusiasts living and working in the Maryland area that are interested in topics related to data science, data analytics, data products, software engineering, machine learning, and other data engineering topics.
When is DAX 2022?
DAX 2022 is scheduled for Saturday, June 4, 2022.
Where is DAX 2022?
DAX 2022 will be held at the UMBC Performing Arts & Humanties Building at UMBC. Food and drinks will be served in the Music Hall.
Who do I contact with additional questions or suggestions?
Please email info@daxconf.org with your questions or suggestions.
